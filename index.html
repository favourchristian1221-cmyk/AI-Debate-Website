Arguments Supporting AI

Personalized Learning

AI adapts lessons to each student's pace.

Affordable Education

Learning with AI costs far less than university tuition.

Accessible Anywhere

Students can learn anytime from anywhere.

Faster Skill Development

AI helps learners focus directly on practical skills.

Arguments Against AI Replacing Universities

Human Mentorship

Teachers inspire, guide, and challenge students.

Critical Thinking

Universities encourage discussion beyond simple answers.

Networking

Campus life builds relationships and leadership skills.

Hands-on Experience

Many careers require physical laboratories and practical work.
